Budget breakdown

Full overview of the cost per participant

Transparancy matters.

The WTMG Tour Festival is a community event. The organising team only wants to make sure that all costs get covered among all participants. Therefore, we created this page to give you a good understanding where the 140 euro participation fee per person comes from.

Full cost overview per participant

Coordination The WTMG Tour Festival is a community event, coordinated by the WTMG team. This budget allows them to organize this event. €30
Insurance Liability and physical damage insurance for volunteers and a basic physical damage insurance for all participants. €3,70

Total price Price per participant for 3 days of WTMG Tour Festival. €140
