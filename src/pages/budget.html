Budget breakdown

Full overview of the cost per participant

Transparancy matters.

The Velotour Festival is a community event. The organising team wants to make sure that all costs get covered among all participants. Therefore, we created this page to give you a good understanding where the 140 euro participation fee per person comes from.

Full cost overview per participant
Cost Description Price
2x dinner Dinner on Saturday and Sunday night.
12 euro/dinner 		€24
2x breakfast Breakfast on Sunday and Monday morning.
6 euro/breakfast 		€12
Campsites Rental of the campsites for 2 nights.
6 euro/day 		€12
Logistics This includes cooking equipment, material, and rental of a small van.
10 euro/day 		€30
Coordination This budget allows the WTMG team to coordinate the organisation of the event and all volunteers. €30
Insurance Liability and physical damage insurance for volunteers and a basic physical damage insurance for all participants. €3,70
Solidarity contribution With 4 extra euro, we support the participation of 5 people who don't have the financial means to participate. €4
VAT 21% VAT. The Velotour Festival is organised by Welcome To My Garden, a not-for-profit project under the legal entity of Slowby, a social enterprise. €24,30
Total price Price per participant for 3 days of Velotour Festival. €140
